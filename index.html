Disneyland

These words were spoken by Walt Disney in the opening moments of the dedication of the original, and in my opinion the best, theme park ever built. Over the past 66 years, Disneyland has become one of the most popular theme parks in the world. Second in annual attendance only to it's sister park, Magic Kingdom at the Walt Disney World Resort, Disneyland hosts almost 20 million guests annually. Though the park has undergone significant changes since opening day, it still maintains all of the wonder and delight that Walt and his team infused it with when first designing and building this timeless attraction. Here I'll share some information about my favorite "Happiest Place on Earth"!
